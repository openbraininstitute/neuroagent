To start a simulation workflow go to Workflows > Category: Simulation. Then choose a scale from Paired Neurons,  Small Microcircuit (up to 20 neurons) or Microcircuit. After this, you will be prompted to select a model for simulation (depending on the scale you chose). You can then configure your simulation. First in Initialize, give it a Name and Description. You can then add different types of Stimuli and Recordings on the left hand side. You can change parameters of the stimuli or the target of the stimulus by creating a new Neuron Set and selcting it as the Neuron Set target of the stimulus. You can additionally make  modifications to the model (neuronal and synaptic modifications). Importantly, any variable of say a Stimulus, Neuron Set, Recording can be scanned over by pressing the plus icon in the field. Once your simulation is configured you can press "Generate simulations". This will generate the simulations in your campaign. You can then select simulations to launch.
