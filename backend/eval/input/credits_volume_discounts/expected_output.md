## Credit Volume Discounts

Yes, bulk credit purchases come with significant discounts. Here's how the pricing scales:

- **No discount**: 1–499 credits at CHF 0.10/credit
- **5% off**: 500–999 credits at CHF 0.095/credit (CHF 48)
- **10% off**: 1,000–4,999 credits at CHF 0.09/credit (CHF 90)
- **15% off**: 5,000–9,999 credits at CHF 0.085/credit (CHF 425)
- **20% off**: 10,000–24,999 credits at CHF 0.08/credit (CHF 800)
- **25% off**: 25,000–49,999 credits at CHF 0.075/credit (CHF 1,875)
- **30% off**: 50,000+ credits at CHF 0.07/credit (CHF 3,500)

The maximum discount is 30% when purchasing 50,000 or more credits, bringing the price per credit down from CHF 0.10 to CHF 0.07.

Note: These are additional credits purchased on top of what your subscription plan (Free, Pro, etc.) already includes each month.

Source: OBI credit pricing (Sanity credits).
