## Additional Credit Pricing

You can purchase additional credits beyond your plan's monthly allowance. Pricing is in CHF with volume discounts:

| Quantity | Price (CHF) | Price per Credit (CHF) | Discount |
|----------|-------------|------------------------|----------|
| 1–499 | 10 | 0.10 | — |
| 500–999 | 48 | 0.095 | 5% |
| 1,000–4,999 | 90 | 0.09 | 10% |
| 5,000–9,999 | 425 | 0.085 | 15% |
| 10,000–24,999 | 800 | 0.08 | 20% |
| 25,000–49,999 | 1,875 | 0.075 | 25% |
| 50,000+ | 3,500 | 0.07 | 30% |

The more credits you buy, the lower the price per credit. Discounts range from 5% to 30%.

Source: OBI credit pricing (Sanity credits).
