## Pricing Plans Overview

We offer several pricing plans to suit different needs:

### Free Plan
- **100 free initial credits** (expires in 1 year, non-transferable)
- **1 GB private free storage** (retained for 3 months after cancellation)
- **Free public storage** for published data & models
- Get a Virtual Lab, join any lab, invite lab members
- AI assistant: 30 free queries per 24h, then 1 credit/query
- Build features: Single neuron (1 credit), Synaptome (1 credit), Ion channel (6 credits)
- Simulate features: E-model, Ion channel, Single cell, Synaptome (1 credit each)

### Pro Plan (CHF 25/month or CHF 275/year)
- **2 GB private free storage**
- **50 free credits/month** (monthly) or **650 free credits/year** (yearly)
- All Free plan features plus reduced costs:
  - Build: Single neuron (0.5 credits), Synaptome (0.5 credits), Ion channel (3 credits)
  - Simulate: Most simulations at 0.5 credits
  - Notebooks: 2 credits/hour (vs 4 for Free)
- Priority support included

### Education Plan
- For courses and classroom teaching
- Seat-based access for instructors & students
- Compute credits included for teaching
- Free storage for course materials
- Contact us for pricing

### Enterprise Plan
- For commercial and organizational use
- Seat-based access for teams
- Scalable compute & storage options
- Custom feature development
- Priority support, onboarding, dedicated account manager
- Contact us for pricing
